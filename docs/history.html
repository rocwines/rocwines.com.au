CHIEF WINE GROWER – LEIGH ELDREDGE

 

As a graduate from the prestigious Roseworthy College (The University of Adelaide) in 1991 alongside the general manager of Tamar Ridge Estate in Tasmania Will Adkins and Wine Purchaser for Qantas Airlines Peter Nickson, Leigh has become one of the Clare Valley's top Wine Growers. With 15 years experience working vintages for Leasingham, Stanley and Annie's Lane Quelltaler he has been acknowledged with a number of prominent accolades for his wines.
